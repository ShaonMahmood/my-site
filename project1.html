Built a medical test and vaccination management system for Vaccina AB

  • Live Demo

Technology Used: Python3, Django, React, Django Rest Framework, Postgresql, Docker, AWS

Build the systems from scratch along with 2 other developers. Worked as a team lead there. The whole project was consists of 3 individual systems named Vaccination, PCR Travel Certificate, Rapid Antibody Test

  • Vaccination system offered vaccination against influenza , pneumococcus , TBE , hepatitis A and B , shingles and chicken pox .
  • Vaccina Travel Certificate provides certificate confirming that you are healthy from covid-19 by performing both antigen tests and PCR tests
  • Vaccina Rapid Test test antibodies in collaboration with the Apoteksgruppen from north to south.
