Built a birthmark checking medical system named Dermly for Docty AB

  • Live Demo

Technology Used: Python3, Django, React, Django Rest Framework, Postgresql, Docker, AWS

Dermly was founded by doctors with an ambition to give more people the opportunity to easily and safely check birthmarks and spots that they are worried about

This system uses a method called Teledermatoscopy to determine the pigmented birthmarks. Respectable Nurses takes the birthmark pictures and send them to the dermatologists to assess through this system. If the examination shows that your birthmark can be dangerous, the case is then referred to a right institution for confirmed care
